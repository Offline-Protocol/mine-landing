## 4. Description of the Service

### 4.1 Overview
MINE is a mobile application that enables users to participate in the Offline Protocol mesh network by operating their device as a mesh relay node. Users earn points ("Points") for their network contributions, complete missions, participate in referral programs, and compete on leaderboards.

### 4.2 Mesh Relay Mining
The core function of the Service is mesh relay mining. When mining is active, your device operates as a relay node within the Offline Protocol mesh network, using Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) to relay encrypted data packets between other mesh participants. Your device advertises its presence and availability to nearby devices, processes encrypted data transit, and earns Points based on your participation. Mining consumes device battery and may use mobile data when internet relay (DORS) is active.

### 4.3 Mission System
The Service offers a mission system that rewards users with Points for completing specified tasks. Mission types include:
- **Daily Missions** — recurring tasks that reset every 24 hours
- **Weekly Missions** — tasks that reset on a weekly cycle
- **Monthly Missions** — tasks that reset on a monthly cycle
- **Geo-Missions** — location-based tasks requiring physical presence within a designated geographic zone (see Section 8)
- **Achievement Missions** — one-time milestone-based accomplishments
- **Streak Missions** — consecutive-day participation rewards

Mission availability, parameters, reward amounts, and types are determined by us at our sole discretion and may be modified, added, or removed at any time without prior notice.

### 4.4 Referral Program
The Service includes a referral program. Each user receives a unique referral code that may be shared with others. When a new user registers using your referral code, both the referrer and the referred user may receive bonus Points, subject to the terms of the referral program then in effect. Referral program terms, bonus amounts, and eligibility criteria are determined by us at our sole discretion and may be modified or discontinued at any time.

### 4.5 Leaderboard
The Service features a competitive leaderboard ranking users by their accumulated Points. Leaderboard visibility means your username, avatar, and Point totals are publicly visible to all users of the Service.

### 4.6 Network Visualization
The Service provides a real-time mesh topology map displaying network activity. This visualization is for informational purposes only and may not reflect the precise or complete state of the mesh network at any given time.

### 4.7 Wallet Integration and Vault Claims
The Service allows you to optionally connect external cryptocurrency wallets (Ethereum via Reown/WalletConnect and Solana via Mobile Wallet Adapter) to your account. Connected wallets may be used to claim token rewards from on-chain vault smart contracts, subject to availability. Wallet integration and vault claim features are described in further detail in Section 9.

## 6. Mesh Networking and Relay

### 6.1 Bluetooth and Network Permissions
The mesh relay feature requires Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) permissions on your device. When mining is active, your device will:
- Advertise its presence to nearby devices also running the Service or other Offline Protocol applications
- Scan for and connect to nearby mesh peers
- Relay encrypted data packets between mesh participants
- Transmit BLE advertisement data including a device identifier (not linked to your personal identity), connection slot availability, device battery level, device uptime, and an application identifier

You may disable Bluetooth access at any time through your device settings, which will disable mesh networking and mining functionality.

### 6.2 Data Relay Disclosure
**By using the mesh relay mining feature, you acknowledge and consent that your device will process and relay encrypted data packets originating from other users and participants in the Offline Protocol mesh network.** You do not have access to the content of these encrypted packets. The data transiting through your device is encrypted and you have no ability or right to decrypt, inspect, modify, or store this transit data. You are acting solely as a passive relay node.

### 6.3 DORS Transport Switching
The Service utilizes Dynamic Opportunistic Relay Switching (DORS), which automatically selects the optimal data transport method from among BLE, WiFi Direct (on supported Android devices), and internet relay. When internet relay is used, data will be transmitted over your device's internet connection, which may consume mobile data and incur charges from your wireless carrier.

### 6.4 Encryption
Mesh relay data may utilize industry-standard encryption protocols for session-based security between peers. While we implement robust encryption, no method of electronic transmission is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security of data in transit.

### 6.5 Resource Consumption
Mesh relay mining consumes device resources including battery power, processing capacity, and potentially mobile data (when DORS selects internet relay). The rate of resource consumption varies based on network activity, number of connected peers, data volume being relayed, and device capabilities. **You are solely responsible for monitoring and managing your device's resource consumption, and we are not liable for any costs, charges, or device degradation arising from your use of the mining feature.**

### 6.6 Network Availability
Mesh networking functionality depends on the proximity, number, and connectivity of other devices participating in the Offline Protocol mesh network. We do not guarantee the availability, reliability, throughput, or performance of the mesh network at any time or in any location.

## 8. Location-Based Features (Geo-Missions)

### 8.1 How Geo-Missions Work
Geo-missions are location-based tasks that require you to be physically present within a designated geographic zone. Zones are generated using a grid-based system (approximately 0.006-degree cells, or roughly 660 meters) with a geofence verification radius of 350 meters. Zones are organized into distance rings from your base location:
- **Close Ring:** 1-3 km
- **Medium Ring:** 3-6 km
- **Far Ring:** 6-10+ km

Point rewards for geo-missions are subject to multipliers based on zone type and distance, with more distant and difficult zones offering higher potential rewards.

### 8.2 Location Data Processing
Location data used for geo-mission verification is processed on your device. Your precise GPS coordinates are not transmitted to our servers for the purpose of geo-mission zone verification. However, general location data may be collected for other Service purposes as described in our Privacy Policy.

### 8.3 Anti-Abuse Limits
To prevent abuse of the geo-mission system, the following limits apply (subject to change at our discretion):
- Maximum of 8 geo-mission completions per day
- Maximum of 3 far-ring geo-mission completions per day
- Zone cooldown periods ranging from 4 to 24 hours after completion, varying by zone type
- Additional limits as we may implement from time to time

### 8.4 Location Accuracy
Geo-mission verification relies on your device's GPS hardware and software. **We do not guarantee the accuracy of GPS positioning, and location accuracy may be affected by environmental factors including but not limited to building obstructions, atmospheric conditions, device hardware limitations, and GPS satellite availability.** We are not responsible for your inability to complete a geo-mission due to location inaccuracy.

### 8.5 Location Spoofing Prohibition
The use of GPS spoofing tools, mock location providers, VPN-based location manipulation, emulators, or any other means to falsify or manipulate your geographic location for the purpose of completing geo-missions is strictly prohibited. Violations will result in immediate account termination and permanent forfeiture of all accumulated Points and rewards without prior notice or appeal.
